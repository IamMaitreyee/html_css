About GHRCEM Pune

G H Raisoni College of Engineering and Management (GHRCEM), Pune, is one of the premier engineering institutions in Maharashtra. The college provides quality education with a focus on academic excellence, innovation, research, and overall personality development of students.

Vision

To become a globally recognized institution by providing quality technical education, promoting research, innovation, and producing socially responsible professionals.

Mission

  • Provide quality education using modern teaching methods.
  • Develop technical, professional, and leadership skills.
  • Encourage research, innovation, and entrepreneurship.
  • Promote ethical values and lifelong learning.

Why Choose GHRCEM?

  • Experienced and Qualified Faculty
  • Well-equipped Laboratories
  • Modern Library and Smart Classrooms
  • Excellent Placement Support
  • Industry Collaboration
  • Sports and Cultural Activities
